Chess

What is chess?

a board game of strategic skill for two players, played on a checkered board. Each player begins the game with sixteen pieces that are moved and used to capture opposing pieces according to precise rules. The object is to put the opponent's king under a direct attack from which escape is impossible ( checkmate ).

Who can learn to play?


Chess is a great game for a wide range of ages.

Parasailing

Parasailing is...

a sport where one or more persons are towed through the air behind a vehicle (usually a boat) while attached to a device similar to a parachute. Participants are lifted into the air, where they are ably to maintain their altitude via winds in a kite-like interaction.
